FitnessFormFixer

As the name may suggest, FitnessFormFixer is a personal web app project that analyzes user's form in a resistance training exercise. Through the integration of neural network and visualization tools, users are able to see themselves performing an exercise and get an assistance to maintain a correct form during the execution of an exercise.
